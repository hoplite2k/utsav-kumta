About Us


Welcome to Hotel Utsav
At Utsav, we are passionate about seafood. Located in the heart of Kumta, we have been proudly serving the freshest catch and exquisite seafood dishes since 1988.

Our Story
Founded by Nagaraj Shet, a seafood enthusiast, Utsav has become synonymous with exceptional seafood dining in Kumta. What started as a humble seafood shack has grown into a renowned destination for seafood lovers.

Our Commitment
At Utsav, we believe in showcasing the natural flavors of seafood through creative and innovative dishes. From succulent grilled fish to decadent prawns, crab dishes, each dish is crafted with the finest ingredients sourced from trusted suppliers. Beyond providing delicious meals, we aim to create a warm and welcoming environment where every guest feels like family. Whether you're celebrating a special occasion or enjoying a casual meal with friends, we promise attentive service and a dining experience to remember.

Visit Us
Indulge in the flavors of the seafood at Utsav. Whether you're craving a seafood feast or looking to enjoy a relaxing meal by the waterfront, our restaurant offers a warm and inviting atmosphere perfect for any occasion.

Thank you for choosing Hotel Utsav. We look forward to welcoming you to a seafood dining experience like no other.
